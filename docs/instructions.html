Instructions for Ascension

---------------------------------------------

Movement:
Up, Down, Left, Right arrows move the player in corresponding direction

Tiles:
Green tiles: These are grass, player can walk on them, no restiction unless there is a rock
Blue tiles: These are water tiles, player cannot walk on them
Brown tiles: These are sand tiles, player can walk on them
Red tiles: These are lava tiles, player cannot walk on them(not implimented yet)
Grey tiles: These are stone tiles, player can walk on all grey and 4 spotted tiles. Other tile is Wall tile, will stop player from moving anymore in that direction
